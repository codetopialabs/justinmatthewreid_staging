About the author

A life given to
the Word.

Daniel Mensah was called to ministry in 1998 and has since served congregations and missionaries across West Africa and beyond. He is a teacher of Scripture, a pastor to the scattered, and the author of four books on prayer, perseverance, and the ordinary grace of God.

He and his wife make their home in Accra, where Living Word Fellowship continues its work of teaching, sending, and writing for the global church.
