About the author

A life given to
the Word.

Justin Matthew Reid was called to ministry in 1998 and has since served congregations and believers in his community and beyond. He is a teacher of Scripture, a preacher of the Word, and the author of published books on prayer, perseverance, and the ordinary grace of God.

He and his wife make their home in Accra, where his ministry continues its work of teaching, preaching, and writing.

Featured release · 2026

Judging Righteous Judgment

A Biblical investigation into righteous judgment

How many times have we heard that only God can judge? If you have wondered what place judgment has in the life of a Christian, this book is for you. Justin Matthew Reid was compelled to advance the discussion about Biblical judgment after observing how judgment has had such a negative stigma attached to it in both the world and the church.

"A timely and necessary investigation designed to illuminate minds which have been formerly poisoned to believe that judgment is always negative and condemning."
